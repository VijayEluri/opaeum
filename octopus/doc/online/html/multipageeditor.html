The Ocl Multipage Editor

The Ocl Multipage Editor is a combination of the Ocl Editor and the BM Viewer. In the first tab the Ocl editor and all its functionality is available. In the second tab you can view the expressions transformed into the BM syntax. If you save the file, it will be saved in the normal syntax. But when you use the 'SaveAs' or 'Print' options, you will be given the choice whether you want to use the normal or the BM syntax.
